Signal Chain

HDP System: Vibration → Signal

The Harmonic Detection Piezo System captures and analyses mechanical harmonics through a precise 5-stage pipeline.

01

Vibration Input

Mechanical vibration applied to the surface where the Piezo Disc is mounted (structural beam, engine casing).

02

Signal Generation

Stress on piezo disc generates a tiny AC electrical signal directly proportional to the applied force.

03

Pre-Amplification

Low-voltage signal fed into high-impedance Op-Amp buffer, improving signal-to-noise ratio.

04

ADC / Arduino

Amplified analogue signal digitised via Arduino's ADC. FFT applied to extract frequency components.

05

Harmonic Detection

Identified harmonic frequencies displayed in real-time or logged for structural health analysis.
