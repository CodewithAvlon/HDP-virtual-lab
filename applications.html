Diverse Applications

🚗

Automotive

Knock detection, airbag deployment, and suspension health monitoring in vehicles.

🏗️

Structural Health

Detecting micro-fractures and fatigue in bridges, pipelines, and buildings.

👟

Smart Wearables

Gait analysis, step counting, and harvesting kinetic energy from human movement.

⚙️

Industrial Diagnostics

Predictive maintenance by monitoring vibration signatures of rotating machinery.

🎸

Instrument Pickups

Capturing string vibrations on acoustic instruments with high fidelity.

🔒

Anti-Theft

Detecting touch or break vibrations on glass and boxes to trigger alarms.

🌊

Underwater Sonar

High-frequency acoustic transducers for depth mapping and object detection.

Medical Ultrasound

Generating and detecting MHz-range ultrasonic waves for diagnostic imaging.
